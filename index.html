Autobiography of Mohammad Yusoph U. Amirodin


Introduction

I was born on the eve of August 09, 2004 in the Mindanao Sanitarium Hospital, Iligan City. Shortly after I was born, my parents stayed at my grandparents' house in our province, Kapatagan, Lanao del Norte, for a short period of time. After my parents moved to Marawi City, I would spend most of my childhood there. As a kid growing up, I have fond memories of playing with my friends outside. We would do all sorts of things that naughty children would come up with, which is another way of saying we played too much outside. I still have the scars on my knees as a result of many accidents that happened in my early childhood years whenever we would play around.
