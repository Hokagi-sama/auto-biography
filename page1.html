Childhood and Hobbies

My childhood was centered around playing with my friends, most of whom are actually my cousins on my mother's side. Needless to say, I was a very playful kid back then. A bad day usually means a day in which I didn't get to play that much outside our house. Although I had toys with me in our house and I did enjoy playing with them, my day would not be complete without messing around with the other kids. We would be playing games like hide-and-seek and all sorts of entertaining activities, which usually involves lots of running.

As a result of moving around too much in my early years, I noticed that I possessed a certain level of athleticism. I found out that I could run faster, jump higher, and play longer than the other kids. As a consequence, I fell inlove with sports. The very first sport I played was basketball (although we used a soccer ball to play basketball). This would then become one of my hobbies going forward. I would also learn to play other sports later, such as badminton, table tennis, and softball.
